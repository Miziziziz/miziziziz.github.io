Affiliate Program

I have an affiliate program where content creators can promote my games and get a cut of the sales revenue.

Program Details

Affiliates receive 50% of sales revenue after discounts, transaction fees, and sales tax.
Payouts have a $50 minimum threshold.
Payouts will be done on the 15th of every month for all sales older than 30 days after this threshold is reached.
You must have a Wise account to receive payouts.

Transactions are processed through Stripe; transaction fees info can be found here: https://stripe.com/pricing/local-payment-methods

I'm based in Florida, which has no sales tax for digital products, but for sales to other countries that might change.

Example sale commission:
Game price: $10
Promo code 10% off: $10 - $1 = $9
Stripe fee 3.4% + 30c: $9 - $0.66 = $8.34
Final commission 50%: $8.34 * 0.5 = $4.17
You get: $4.17


Signing Up

If you would like to promote my games and get a cut of the sales revenue, email me at miz@deeproot.dev with the subject "Interested in joining your Affiliate Program".

In your email include a link to your socials where you plan to promote (e.g. Youtube Twitch Tiktok).
